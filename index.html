Psi Beta Chapter of Theta Tau

The nation's oldest and foremost fraternity for engineers.

Theta Tau is a nation-wide professional engineering fraternity. At Psi Beta chapter, we strive to uphold an unbroken tradition of fraternal fellowship and brotherhood.

Established at The University of Texas at Austin in 1991, the Psi Beta chapter represents a diverse, energetic group of about 70 students, both male and female, representing all disciplines, united by a common interest in engineering and the sciences.
